Recipe

Chocolate cake

"Here is a fast and easy version of the classic Chocolate Cake recipe. Enjoy"



Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Black chocolate: 200g

Sugar: 200g

Butter: 200g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80g

Instruction

1

Melt the chocolate and the buttter together

2

Mix sugar, eggs and the flour

3

Mix everything to get a smoothe dough

4

Bake in the oven
